Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about joining and participating in Research Club.

No prior experience is required! We welcome students at all levels and provide comprehensive training and mentorship to help you develop your research skills.

Topics are chosen through a collaborative process. You can propose your own ideas or select from our curated list of research areas. We help you refine and develop your research questions.

We guide you through the publication process, from manuscript preparation to submission. We have partnerships with various journals and platforms to help you get published.

Teams are formed based on research interests, complementary skills, and availability. You can work individually or in groups of 2-4 students, depending on the project scope.

We have a structured timeline with regular check-ins. Most projects require 5-10 hours per week, with key milestones throughout the academic year.

Absolutely! Solo projects are welcome and can be very rewarding. You'll still receive mentorship and support from our team and advisors.
